## Overview

Mature the PMO as Code specification from a well-tested draft to a 1.0
standard a stranger could implement: normative execution mapping backed by
live evidence, risk lifecycle semantics, the operations kind, an expanded
conformance suite, and a published versioning policy.

## Scope

SPEC.md, the conformance suite, and the standard's own versioning
governance, through 1.0. Engine implementation is out of scope.
