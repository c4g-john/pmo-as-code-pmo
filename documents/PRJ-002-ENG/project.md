## Overview

Take docassert from a fast-moving 0.x tool to a stable 1.0: the features the
portfolio itself has discovered it needs (per-project bridge mapping, risk
disposition, an operations document kind), a public stability promise, and
the quality floor to back it. Covers the docassert package, the GitHub
Action, and the Homebrew and PyPI distribution channels.

## Scope

The docassert package, docassert-action, and the PyPI and Homebrew
channels, through the 1.0 release. Spec text and site content are out of scope.
