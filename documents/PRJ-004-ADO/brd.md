## Purpose

The ecosystem is built and dogfooded; its business value is zero until other
people use it. This project converts engineering credibility into adoption,
honestly: no claims a reader cannot verify against a live repository.

## Business Requirements

- **ADO-BR-001**: The business shall launch publicly, with announcements published on LinkedIn and Hacker News once the engine is stable at 1.0.
- **ADO-BR-002**: The business shall keep the public site complete with respect to released features, with zero released engine features undocumented at launch.
- **ADO-BR-003**: The business shall reach 10 or more external adopters, measured monthly by template forks plus action dependents, with the count method published.
- **ADO-BR-004**: The business shall demonstrate self-governance publicly, linking the live portfolio dashboard from the site.

## Out of Scope

Paid marketing; conference talks; commercial support offerings; certification
programs.
