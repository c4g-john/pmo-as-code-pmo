## Overview

Risks to a credible launch and real adoption.

## Risks

- **ADO-RISK-001** (threatens: ADO-BR-001): Launch attention is a lottery; the announcement may land flat. Probability: high. Impact: medium. Owner: johntanner. Response: the verifiable self-governance story differentiates; a flat launch still leaves evergreen documentation and SEO in place for slower discovery.
- **ADO-RISK-002** (threatens: ADO-BR-002): Documentation drifts as the engine keeps releasing. Probability: high. Impact: medium. Owner: johntanner. Response: feature-parity acceptance runs against the changelog at launch; post-launch parity transfers to OPS as a service item.
- **ADO-RISK-003** (threatens: ADO-BR-003): Early adopters hit setup friction and leave silently. Probability: medium. Impact: high. Owner: johntanner. Response: quarterly end-to-end template walkthrough from the README alone; issue templates lower the reporting bar.
